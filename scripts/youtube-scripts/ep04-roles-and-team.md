# Episode 04 — Roles & Team Management: Giving Everyone the Right Access
**Duration target:** 5–6 min
**Style:** Show each role view, then walk through the invite flow

---

## INTRO (0:00–0:25)

[Screen: director console, header showing role chip]

> "A live event is a team sport. Director, stage managers, AV engineers, interpreters, registration desk — everyone needs the right information, and only the right information. CueDeck handles this with six distinct roles, each with a tailored view and a specific set of permissions."

---

## THE SIX ROLES (0:25–2:30)

[Screen: open role switcher or show different browser tabs per role]

**Director**
> "The Director sees everything. Full session list, full context panel, all action buttons — Set Ready, Go Live, Hold, End, nudge, delay cascade, signage, broadcast. This is the person running the show."

[Screen: switch to Stage role]

**Stage**
> "Stage Managers get the session list with status badges and the ability to mark speaker arrivals. They can transition sessions — Set Ready, Call Speaker, Go Live. They don't see financials or settings."

[Screen: switch to AV role]

**AV**
> "AV engineers see what's LIVE right now, what's coming next, and the recording/streaming flags on each session. They don't need the full programme — just what's relevant to their setup."

[Screen: switch to Interp role]

**Interpretation**
> "Interpretation booths get a clean queue view — current session, next session, language flags. If a session has interpretation enabled, it shows here. No clutter."

[Screen: switch to Reg role]

**Registration**
> "Registration desk sees who's on stage, what's next, and can check in arrivals. Simple, focused."

[Screen: switch to Signage role]

**Signage**
> "Signage operators manage the digital display panel — adding and configuring screens, changing modes, uploading sponsor logos. They don't touch sessions at all."

---

## INVITING TEAM MEMBERS (2:30–4:00)

[Screen: Director view, show Invite Team button or team section]

> "To add someone to your event, go to the team panel. Click Invite Operator.
>
> Enter their email address. Choose their role. Hit Send Invite.
>
> They get an email with a magic link. They click it, set their password if they're new, and they're in — automatically scoped to your event with the role you assigned.
>
> The invite is tied to you as the event director. If they're already a CueDeck user they just get access to your event instantly."

[Action: send an invite to a test email, show the welcome modal]

---

## ROLE PERMISSIONS REFERENCE (4:00–4:45)

[Screen: Help → Quick Reference → Role Permissions]

> "Not sure what a role can do? Hit the question mark in the header, then Quick Reference. You'll see a full table of every state transition and which roles can trigger it.
>
> Directors get everything. Stage managers get transitions. AV and Interp are read-only for sessions. This is your field reference during production."

[Action: open help → quick reference, zoom in on role permissions table]
