# Episode 01 — Welcome to CueDeck: The Production Console for Live Events
**Duration target:** 4–5 min
**Style:** Energetic overview, no login required — show the polished UI

---

## INTRO (0:00–0:25)

[Screen: cuedeck.io homepage hero]

> "If you've ever had to manage a live conference — keeping track of which speaker is on stage, whether they're running late, what the AV team is doing, what the interpreter room needs — you know how chaotic it can get.
>
> CueDeck is a realtime production console built specifically for live events. One URL, every role, everything synced instantly. Let me show you what it can do."

---

## WHAT IS CUEDECK (0:25–1:10)

[Screen: switch to app.cuedeck.io login page, then pan around the console UI after login]

> "CueDeck lives entirely in the browser — no app to install, no setup wizard that takes three hours. You sign in, pick your event, and you're in.
>
> What you see here is the Production Console — this is the director view. On the left you've got a live session list for today's event. Each card shows the session title, the room, the speaker, the planned time, and the current status.
>
> On the right is the context panel — this is where you take action. Go live, put a session on hold, call the speaker to stage, apply a delay. Every action is a single click, and it propagates to every connected device in under a second."

---

## THE ROLES (1:10–1:55)

[Screen: show the role bar at top, mention each role]

> "CueDeck has six roles, each with exactly the right level of access for the job.
>
> The Director has full control — they see everything, do everything.
> Stage managers can transition sessions and mark speaker arrivals.
> AV can see what's live and what's coming.
> Interpretation rooms get a dedicated view with the session queue.
> Registration sees who's next on stage.
> And the Signage role manages the digital displays around the venue.
>
> Everyone gets the right information at the right time — no more group chats, no more walkie-talkie chaos."
